On the studio

We are a small workshop of software craftspeople orbiting a quiet sun. Our concern is the everyday — the windows you keep open, the logs you stare at, the focus you keep losing. We build tools that earn their place on your desktop by being quiet, fast, and considerate.

No subscriptions to forget. No telemetry to dread. Just instruments that do one thing carefully, transmit nothing home, and then get out of the way.
